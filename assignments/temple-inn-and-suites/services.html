At The Temple Inn & Suites we provide a variety of services and ameneties that are meant to help you have the most relaxed and enjoyable experience as possible. These services are available to anyone who is willing to abide by our terms and policies and follow church standards. Our services include:

Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities
We provide changing rooms for those who just need a place to change out of their travel clothes into church clothes. Imagine not having to change in a bathroom somewhere into your Sunday best. Our changing rooms provide hanging hooks for hanging your clothes on as you are changing to prevent wrinkles and soiling. We have single person changing rooms as well as family changing rooms available.
A playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for children of patrons
For families who travel to attend the temple, you have a place for your kids to stay while you attend the temple. This allows you to attend the temple together as a couple instead of having to go separately while one of you stays with the kids. The playroom is equipped with fun and educational toys for the kids to enjoy and our certified care staff will give your kids a safe and friendly environment.
Long-term stay accomodations
We have a variety of available kitchenette suites available for full-time temple missionaries. These allow you to reside in an environment that is built on church standards and allows you to be near the temple in a cost friendly location. For more information on available options please visit our Long-Term Stay page.
Temple history themed reception and sitting area
Imagine being able to learn about the temple you will be serving in right where you stay. Each one of our locations has a reception and sitting area that is dedicated to providing the history of the local temple you will be attending. This includes information about when it was built, who it was dedicated by, and any stories surrounding the building of the temple or of the temple itself.
Wedding reception hall
We have an available wedding reception hall that can be customized with a simple catering menu if desired. There is also access to a kitchen for food preperation and serving to allow you to bring your own food in for serving. The reception hall can comfortably seat 100 guests at large round tables and has an available dance floor. For more information on our wedding reception packages please visit our reception packages page.
Family search center
In our available family search center you will have access to computers to log into familysearch.org and work on your family history while you are here. There are available family history consultants to help you with any questions and to help get you started. There are also family ordinance card services available to make taking your own family names with you easier.

We also have a variety of free amenities for those who are staying with us in our rooms. These include:

  • Breakfast every morning from 5-11 AM
  • Exercise/workout rooms
  • Indoor pool
  • Wi-Fi high-speed internet access
  • Smart TVs in each room
  • Parking
  • Shuttles to and from the temple
  • Family history conultation and family ordinance card services
