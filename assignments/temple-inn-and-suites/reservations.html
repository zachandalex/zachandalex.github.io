Reservations

At The Temple Inn & Suites we aim to provide the best experience for your stay. We look forward to serving you when you stay with us. Now that you are ready to book a reservation please review our room type options and fill out the form below. Once you submit the form you will receive a confirmation number. Please save this number for your records as you will need it for any communications about your reservation.

Our rooms are all tastefully decorated to fit with the local temple you will be visiting.

If you are interested in our long-term stay options for full or part-time temple missionaries, please visit our long term stay page for more information.

Single Queen Size Bed

Our single queen size bed rooms are ideal for the individual who is traveling or for a couple who wants to be frugal with their costs.

Two Queen Size Beds

Our double queen rooms offer two queen size beds. Ideal for a small family or friends traveling together and sharing the cost.

King Size Bed

Our king size beds offer more room to spread out. Ideal for that individual who just likes their space or for a couple.

One Room Suite

We offer a limited number of one room suites for that family who wants to save even more by being able to prepare their own food while here. This is also ideal for groups traveling together.
