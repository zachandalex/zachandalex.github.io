Wedding Receptions

Every bride and groom wants to feel special on their wedding day. What better way to do that than with a personalized wedding reception. At The Temple Inn & Suites we are proud to offer wedding reception spaces to fit your needs. You can choose to do the entire reception yourself including food and setup or you can choose from one of our available packages to allow us to take the stress out of your big day.

There are two reception hall sizes to choose from. Our Smith reception hall will seat up to 100 guests. Our Young reception hall can seat up to 200 guests. Each hall has a dance floor space and a DJ or band space.

Tables are large rounds that will seat 10 guests at each. We have a variety of table cloths in multiple colors for you to choose from. We also have available matching chair slip covers. The bride and groom table is a rectangular table with throne style chairs to help the special guests of honor feel like royalty.
